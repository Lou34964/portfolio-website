About Me

My Name is Chad Snyder. I was born in Payson Utah. In my earlyest years i lived in Springville Utah, at the age of 5 my parents built thier beautiful home in Mapleton. Now i have lived here with my parent for many years.

At about the age of 14, I began to gain intrest in Computer Science(cs). I started learing how to computers worked. I dreamed of building my own software, so i started looking into how i could do so. Soon I learned how to write my own batch command files (.bat). I played around with this for a long time and had a lot of fun with it. But i thought 'there must be more'

High School

Early into Junior High School I had become familiar with computers and their basic functions but there was something more I could learn. High School was my ticket. In High School I learned the basics of JavaScript and Java. Later we learned C#.
C# was my best friend it allowed me to build my own user interactive software and I soon began building powerful programs that could do thing I thought was impossible.

My First Real World Project

Before coming to Lambda School I worked for a company that manufactured Diamonds. I saw a need for a systems for tracking information of productivity. The company had good systems for Company Wide tracking but they were missing something. My team needed something to track our day to day production I began working on software that tracked info and calculated totals and other info that took valuable time out of the day. Other systems fallowed and in total 3 different application I built are still being used and are very powerful.
